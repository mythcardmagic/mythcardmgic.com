How much will it cost me to create a Quick-E-Card?

NOTHING! There is no cost, no subscription and your video is ready in seconds.

The app does contain adverts, but they run while the video is being created, so you would be waiting anyway.

Can anyone receive my Quick-E-Card?

Yes, Quick-E-Card creates a video that can be shared anywhere. The recipient does not need to have the app, just the ability to receive and watch your creation. The Quick-E-Card can be shared via any social media and communication platform.

Are there any Limitations?

Unfortunately due to the power required to create and render a video from your images and personal message, it is recommended that your phone or device has at least 6gb of RAM. It has been known that 4gb RAM devices can run the app, but not always.
